TECHCORP PRODUCT FAQ — CloudSync Pro v3.2

Q: What operating systems does CloudSync Pro support?
A: CloudSync Pro is compatible with Windows 10 and above, macOS 11 (Big Sur) and above, and Ubuntu 20.04 LTS and above. Mobile clients are available for iOS 15+ and Android 10+. Older operating systems are not supported and may experience instability.

Q: How much storage do I get on the free plan?
A: The free tier includes 15 GB of cloud storage. This is shared across all connected devices and file types. Storage usage can be monitored from the account dashboard under Settings > Storage.

Q: What are the paid plan options?
A: We offer three paid tiers:
- Starter: 100 GB for $4.99/month
- Professional: 1 TB for $12.99/month  
- Business: 5 TB + admin controls for $29.99/month per team
Annual billing offers a 20% discount on all plans.

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Visit app.cloudsync.com/reset, enter your registered email address, and follow the link sent to your inbox. The reset link expires in 30 minutes. If you do not receive the email, check your spam folder. For accounts with SSO (Single Sign-On) enabled, password resets must be handled through your organisation's identity provider.

Q: Is my data encrypted?
A: Yes. All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3 and at rest using AES-256 encryption. Encryption keys are managed by CloudSync and rotated automatically every 90 days. Enterprise customers on the Business plan can opt for customer-managed encryption keys (CMEK).

Q: What is the maximum file size I can upload?
A: Free and Starter plan users can upload individual files up to 5 GB. Professional and Business plan users can upload files up to 50 GB. Batch uploads are supported for up to 500 files at a time.

Q: How do I contact support?
A: Support is available via live chat (Monday–Friday, 9 AM–6 PM IST), email at support@cloudsync.com (response within 24 hours), and the community forum at forum.cloudsync.com. Business plan users have access to a dedicated account manager and 24/7 phone support.

Q: Can I share files with people who don't have a CloudSync account?
A: Yes. You can generate a shareable link for any file or folder. Links can be set to view-only or allow editing. You may also set an expiry date and password-protect links. Guest users accessing shared links do not need a CloudSync account.
